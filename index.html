To use YT Downloader 

  • Enter the link to the desired movie (it can be shortened) in the URL field and click ENTER.
  • Then select the desired format - Audio or Video.
  • Wait, the application is preparing your video!
  • When the file is ready, the download button will appear.

  • Enjoi!
